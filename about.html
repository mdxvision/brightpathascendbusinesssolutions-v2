Brightpath Ascend Business Solutions structures businesses for one purpose: access to capital.

Most businesses fail to secure funding because they weren't built correctly from the start. Wrong entity type. Missing credibility markers. No business credit file. Lenders reject what they can't verify.

We reverse-engineer what lenders require and build it into your business foundation. 411 listings. EIN optimization. Business credit bureaus. Trade lines. Vendor accounts. Every element that separates funded businesses from rejected applications.

We don't just form entities.

Every fundability requirement.

Entity Foundation

LLC or Corporation formation with optimal state selection. EIN acquisition. Registered agent. Operating agreements. The legal structure lenders trust.

Credibility Architecture

Credit Infrastructure

D&B DUNS number. Experian Business file. Equifax Business file. Trade lines. Vendor accounts. A complete business credit profile.

0% intro credit lines. No personal guarantee options. Term loans. SBA preparation. The capital your business deserves.
